Lasanga

Description

An easy lasanga recipe with a Mexican twist to change things up.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 oz taca seasoning
  • 14 oz can diced tomatoes
  • 10 6 in corn tortillas
    • <1i>1 cup salsa
  • 1/2 shredded cheese

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 175 degree C
  2. In a large skillet over medium low heat, brown the beef, and mix in the tomatoes and seasoning.
  3. Line a baking dish with half the tortillas, than spoon the beef mix into the dish
  4. Line the remaining tortillas over the beef mix, than spread salso and cheese over the top
  5. Bake for 20-30 minutes, or until cheese is melted
